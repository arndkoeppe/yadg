yadg version 4.2

Warning

yadg-4.2 is not yet released.

Developed at Empa - Materials Science and Technology, in Dübendorf.

New features since v4.1 are:

  • chromdata parser for parsing integrated chromatography data,

Bug fixes and other modifications include:

  • the drycal filetypes in yadg.parsers.flowdata should now work for overnight experiments;

  • the chromtrace parser now focuses on parsing chromatography traces only, with trace integration deprecated in favour of dgpost;

  • the flowdata parser now no longer creates a default "flow" entry in derived data;

  • the calfile and calib parameters have been deprecated in favour of dgpost;

  • the metadata in chromdata and chromtrace was modified to only include true metadata as opposed to sample data;
