Citing yadg

yadg is currently being developed at the Materials for Energy Conversion lab at Empa, Dübendorf. If you find yadg useful, please cite our recent paper [Kraus2022b], or our previous work where yadg was first introduced [Kraus2022a]. We extend no warranty, neither express nor implied.

Papers related to yadg

Kraus2022b

Kraus, P.; Vetsch, N.; Battaglia, C. yadg: yet another datagram, Journal of Open Source Software 2022, 7, 4166, DOI: https://doi.org/10.21105/joss.04166

Kraus2022a

Kraus, P.; Wolf, E.H.; Prinz, C.; Bellini, G.; Trunschke, A.; Schlögl, R. Towards automation of operando experiments: a case study in contactless conductivity measurements, Digital Discovery 2022, advance article, DOI: https://doi.org/10.1039/D1DD00029B
